The Best Movies According to Kevin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pacific Rim

It's a mecha sci-fi movie about godzila-like aliens invading our planet, while humanity defends using the oversized robots as defenders of the planet.

Avatar

Mankind started invading other planets for precious metals and other resource, but they started destroying the forests which are the natural habitat of the locals. I just love everything about this movie.

The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen

It's an old movie, but i was always fascinated by it as a kid. Although I'm not really sure why i like it.
